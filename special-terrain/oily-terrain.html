Oily Terrain

Oily terrain is covered in a layer of slippery, flammable oil. When you enter oily terrain for the first time in a round or end your turn there, you must spend -4 poise or you become unbalanced. For each space you are shoved into oily terrain, you are moved two spaced instead of one. A character knocked-down in oily terrain becomes oiled.

Oily terrain is replaced by burning terrain and becomes burning terrain if it is in the area of a fire area or line ability or if it contains a burning character. If one space of oiled terrain becomes burning, all contiguous spaces also become burning. Otherwise, oiled terrain lasts indefinitely.
