Icy Terrain

Icy terrain is covered in a sheet of slippery ice. When you enter icy terrain for the first time in a round or end your turn there, you must spend -4 poise or you become unbalanced. For each space you are shoved into icy terrain, you are moved two spaced instead of one. A character knocked-down in icy terrain becomes chilled.

A space of icy terrain included in a fire skill is transformed into wet terrain. Icy terrain thaws after at least a minute, but could last indefinitely depending on the weather.
