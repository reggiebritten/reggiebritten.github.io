Climbing

Climbing up an easy structure like a ladder typically doesn’t require a roll, but the GM will usually call for a brawn roll to climb up a rope, tree, or cliff or other surface. You can use a movement skill to climb, but you must spend two points of movement for every space you climb. In general, you can’t use actions while climbing, although the GM may make exceptions. You can’t defend against attacks while you’re climbing; you must yield to any attack.
