Sight & Darkness

The GM will describe the amount of light in each area as either bright, dim, or dark.

  • Daylight, torches, and campfires all cast bright light, which imposes no penalties or modifications. On a clear day with no obstructions, you can see out to 3 miles or 5km away.

  • An starry night in an open field or the border of a fire may be dim light. All skills suffer a bane when targeting a character in darkness.

  • A dark area, such as a the depths of a cave or a midnight forest, is devoid of light. Most characters can’t see in darkness, meaning you can’t even target other characters with skills. If you move more than one space in a turn without being able to see where you are going, you risk bumping into something, making noise and suffering stress.
