Flashbacks

When your character achieves certain goals, the GM will reward you with the opportunity for a flashback. A flashback is a chance to explore a detail of your character’s past. When you resolve a flashback, discuss with the GM what detail you would like to explore.

If the GM agrees that the proposed embellishment is reasonable in the context of the character’s existing background, then the player may spend a point of fate to add the embellishment to their character’s background. In general, embellishing a character’s background is easier if fewer details have already been established, and if the detail being added is consistent with the background details that the character already has. The GM should be more permissive of characters who have few background details already established or few that have aided them on their quest so far. Everyone comes from somewhere, and all characters are entitles to backgrounds that help them achieve their goals.
