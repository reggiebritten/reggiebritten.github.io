Insight

Canny, Clever, Astute? Insight represents a character’s awareness, acuity, and cunning. Insightful characters can get the drop on hidden enemies or traps before they can strike, and spot key details that others might miss. Insightful character’s aren’t necessarily more intelligent, just better at noticing details and recognizing opportunities. In combat, insightful characters excel at exploiting a foe’s weakness and creating opportunities for their allies to deliver devastating attacks.

  • Ability rolls to find or notice hidden details or characters are based on your insight.
  • A character’s base focus is equal to five plus five times their insight. Focus is often used for skills that require foresight and observation.
  • A character adds their insight to the range of any ranged skill they use.
  • A character’s action challenges have their TN reduced by their insight, making critical and fated effects more likely.
