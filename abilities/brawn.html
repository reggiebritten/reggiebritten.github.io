Brawn

Brawn represents a characterâ€™s physical strength and toughness. Brawny characters can use their might to overcome obstacles and haul more equipment. In combat, brawny characters excel at going toe-to-toe with enemies in melee combat and outlasting their enemies.

  • Challenges that involve running, swimming, climbing, jumping or moving heavy objects are based on brawn.

  • Your base momentum is equal to five plus five times your brawn. Momentum is used for skills that benefit from raw brute force.

  • Add your brawn to the yield damage of your melee attacks.

  • Your armor reduces the damage you take from enemy attacks, and will usually equal your brawn.

  • Your carrying capacity, measured in bulk, is equal to three plus five times your brawn.
