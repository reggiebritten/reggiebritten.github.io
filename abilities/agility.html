Agile characters can gracefully elude enemies, and balance, squeeze, or tumble their way into areas others would struggle to reach. In combat, agile characters excel quickly finishing off worn-down foes and dashing to the right place at the right time.

  • Rolls that involve moving quickly, carefully, and stealthily are based on agility.

  • Your base poise is equal to five plus five times your agility. Poise is used for skills that benefit from finesse and precision.

  • Add your agility to the threat of all your attacks.

  • Your speed increases the distance you can more, and will usually equal your agility.
