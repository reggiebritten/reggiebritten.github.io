Primalist

Primalists call upon the the powers of animals, plants, and the land itself.

  • Beastmaster WIP: A crafty scout who fights along side an animal companion and draws on their guile.

  • Shapeshifter WIP: A savage that wholly transforms into animals, plants, and primal elementals.

  • Geomancer WIP: A sage whose communion with the land shapes their every step.
