Rogue

Rogues have a knack for fighting in the way that their enemies least expect.

  • Assassin: A deadly shadow who uses stealth and surprise to ambush enemies.

  • Trickster WIP: A dirty fighter who debilitates foes and takes advantage of their weakness.

  • Bard WIP: A weaver of songs and keeper of legends who recites verses to bolster allies and confuse enemies.
