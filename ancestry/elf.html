Elf

Elven Swiftness: You gain +1 speed.

Sense Magic: As an action, you can attune your senses to the presence of magic until you move. The GM will tell you if there is any magic within 5 paces, other than that associated with you and your travelling companions. The GM only reveals the existence of such magic, not its location or nature.

Woodland Harmony: You recover 1 stress during any day you spent mostly outdoors.

Elven Frailty: Your stress tolerance is reduced by 3.
