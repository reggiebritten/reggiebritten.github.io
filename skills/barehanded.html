Barehanded

A barehanded skill requires the use of one free hand, and typically can’t be used while wielding a separate equipment/weapon in each hand. A barehanded skill may be used while wielding one two-handed weapon, but you cannot use a barehanded skill white concentrating on a two-handed weapon’s skill and vice versa.

Cunning: The target becomes grabbed while you concentrate, you take one of the target’s weapons, or you remove a non-persistent condition from the target.
