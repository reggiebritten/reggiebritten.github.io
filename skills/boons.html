Boons

Each boon that your skill receives increases the number of dice you roll for the skill (if any) by one. Each boon also increases the skill’s threat by +4 if it is an attack. If the skill doesn’t roll dice and isn’t an attack, then it is not affected by boons. If an effect gives your whole skill a boon, then it gains a boon against each target. However, other effects might only give your skill a boon against specific targets, not all of them.
