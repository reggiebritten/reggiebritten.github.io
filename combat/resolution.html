Order of Resolution [WIP]

Some skills, attack, support or otherwise, will offer the user, the target, or both a choice about what happens. Unless specified otherwise, a skill’s effects require the character using it to choose first before anything else happens and before the target decides to yield or defend an attack.

However, an attack’s yield requires the target to choose first, then the attacker may choose any additional effects afterwards.

  • First, the skill user chooses the target(s) of the skill and decides on any choices offered by the effects.
  • Second, any characters may choose to intervene with reactions, where the first character to declare their reaction may use it first.
  • Third, the target chooses from the choices offered, including defening or yielding an attack.
  • Fourth, reactions may be used again.
  • Fifth, the user chooses from the options offered by the the yield.
  • Finally, the user applies any fated effects they may have earned.
