Hindering Maneuvers

Using most maneuvers while engaged with the enemy is risky. Whenever you use a maneuver in an enemy character’s zone-of-control, that enemy can hinder you. When you are hindered, you immediately lose -2 poise, -2 momentum, and -2 focus. Hindering is not an attack; there is no threat or yield and your armor doesn’t reduce the loss. You may enter a hostile character’s zone of control without being hindered, but using a maneuver to move from within an enemy zone of control allows that enemy to hinder you. A skill with the careful tag can’t be hindered. Actions and reactions also can’t be hindered, even if they let you move. However far you move, each enemy can only hinder you once per maneuver.
